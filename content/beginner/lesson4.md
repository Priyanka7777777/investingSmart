## What Are Shares (Stocks)?

When you buy a **share**, you:
- Own a small part of a company
- Directly invest in that business
- Your returns depend on how that company performs

### Pros
- Higher return potential  
- Full control over investments  

### Cons
- Requires time and understanding  
- Prices can fluctuate a lot  

**Best for:**  
People willing to learn and track businesses.

---

## What Are Mutual Funds?

A **mutual fund**:
- Collects money from many investors
- Is managed by a professional fund manager
- Invests in many companies on your behalf

Instead of choosing stocks yourself,
you trust an expert to do it for you.

### Pros
- Diversification (risk is spread)
- Less effort
- Beginner-friendly

### Cons
- Lower control
- Small management fees

**Best for:**  
Beginners and long-term investors.
