## What Is Saving?

Saving means:
- Keeping money safe
- Having easy access when needed
- Protecting against emergencies

Common saving options:
- Savings account
- Fixed deposits (FD)
- Recurring deposits (RD)
- Cash

Saving is **important**, but it has one limitation:

> It does not grow your money meaningfully.

---

## What Is Investing?

Investing means:
- Putting money into assets that grow over time
- Allowing your money to work for you
- Accepting short-term ups and downs for long-term growth

Common investing options:
- Mutual funds
- Stocks
- ETFs
- Bonds (later)

Investing focuses on **growth**, not just safety.
