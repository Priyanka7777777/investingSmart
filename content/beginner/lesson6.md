## First, understand this clearly

---

## What Is a Demat Account?

**Demat** means **Dematerialised**.

A Demat account:
- Holds your shares and mutual funds
- Stores them in **electronic form**
- Works like a **digital locker** for investments

Earlier, people used physical share certificates.  
Today, everything is digital.

> üí° You cannot invest without a Demat account.

---

## What Is a Trading Account?

A Trading account:
- Is used to **buy and sell** investments
- Connects you to the stock exchange
- Acts like a **transaction account**

Think of it as:
> The place where buying and selling happens.

---

## Simple Analogy (Very Important)

- **Trading account** ‚Üí Placing the order  
- **Demat account** ‚Üí Wardrobe where clothes are stored  
- **Bank account** ‚Üí Payment source  

All three work together.

## Do You Need Both?

Yes.

| Account | Purpose |
|------|--------|
| Demat | Stores investments |
| Trading | Buys & sells |
| Bank | Pays & receives money |

Most apps today open all three together.

---

## How Investing Happens (Step-by-Step)

1. You place a buy order using a trading app  
2. Money is deducted from your bank account  
3. Investment is credited to your Demat account  
4. You become the owner  

Selling works in reverse.
